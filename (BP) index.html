BUKO PANDAN PASTEELIAS RECIPE!





INGREDIENTS - Butter Milk Powder – 150g (2cups) Powder Sugar – 70g (1cup) Evaporated Milk – 70g (1/2 cup) EZ’s Best Flavocol Buko Pandan - 8g(4tsp)






INSTRUCTIONS - 1. Combine milk powder and sugar in a bowl, then sift together. 2. Bind with milk and EZ's Best Buko Pandan Flavocol until well blended. Form into desired size and roll in desiccated coconut, then wrap in glassine paper. (https://ezbake.com/blogs/ezbake-creations/buko-pandan-pastillas-balls)
